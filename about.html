Welcome to Blue Tech Base!


Our mission is to create educational climate-tech content which helps develop the sector. We are addressing key industry issues by creating technical multimedia climate-tech content for undergraduates, postgraduates, start-up teams, founders, university professors and investors.



How can we help?


Undergraduates, graduates and postgraduates

We'll be bringing you guidance, and providing more info about the technical frontiers of the space, transitioning into climate-tech, finding co-founders, and building companies in the space. You'll be able to connect with others and access a support system to help you along this journey.
