Welcome to Blue Tech Base!


Our mission is to create educational climate-tech content which helps develop the sector. We are addressing key industry issues by creating technical multimedia climate-tech content for undergraduates, postgraduates, start-up teams, founders, university professors and investors.



How can we help?


Undergraduates, graduates and postgraduates

We'll be bringing you guidance and providing more info about the technical frontiers of the space. Whether you're transitioning into climate-tech, looking for co-founders, or building companies in the space, you'll find something just right for you here. Alongside all of this, you'll be able to connect with others and access a support system to assist you on your journey.


Investors

Blue Tech Base will connect you to more climate-tech start-ups, led by energetic and knowledgeable young founders. These teams will know where potential markets lie and improve your chances of betting on the right people. We'll be sharing information about promising teams and a database of companies to invest in.


Start-up founders and teams

As you scale your operations or seek product-market fit, you need talent. Blue Tech Base will give you access to a host of promising candidates to join your team. We will provide a database of individuals who can help you move faster.


Academia

The Blue Tech Base ecosytem can help spread the message of your work. As a result, our content and network can you help you gain the fundraising you need. We can also connect you with promising young researchers and help take your work out of the university environment.
